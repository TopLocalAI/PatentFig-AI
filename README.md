## What is PatentFig AI?

PatentFig AI is an AI-powered platform that helps inventors, patent attorneys, and IP professionals create professional patent drawings without any CAD or illustration skills. Whether you need a flowchart, an exploded view, a mechanical assembly, or a block diagram for your next patent application, PatentFig AI generates filing-ready drawings in seconds.

> No more outsourcing patent illustrations for weeks. No more paying hundreds of dollars per figure.

| Mode | Description |

### USPTO & WIPO Compliance

- Fully compliant with **37 CFR §1.84** drawing standards
- Pure **black-and-white line art** with solid, uniformly thick lines
- Proper **reference numerals** and leader lines
- Correct margins, paper size, and view labels (FIG. 1, FIG. 2, ...)
- Meets the standards of **USPTO**, **EPO**, **WIPO (PCT)**, **JPO**, and **CNIPA**

### Vector Export for Patent Filing

- Export to **PDF**, **PNG**, **TIFF**, and **SVG**
- **DXF export** for CAD workflows
- Fully editable vector output compatible with **Adobe Illustrator**, **Inkscape**, and **AutoCAD**
- Scale infinitely without quality loss
- 300 / 600 / 1000 DPI resolution options

### Drawing Types Supported

- **Flowcharts & Block Diagrams** — for software and process patents

- **Cross-Sections** — for internal structural details
- **Perspective & Isometric Views** — for 3D product visualization
- **Circuit Diagrams** — for electronic patents
- **Chemical Structures** — for pharmaceutical and material patents
